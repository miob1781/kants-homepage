At the heart of my philosophical project is what I call the "critical" approach. I have applied this to both theoretical and practical philosophy, bringing the metaphysics of nature and of morals to completion.

You may also know me for my engagement in current intellectual debates in the spirit of enlightenment and societal progress. I have argued for free speech, the freedom and independence of philosophical faith, republicanism, and eternal peace.
